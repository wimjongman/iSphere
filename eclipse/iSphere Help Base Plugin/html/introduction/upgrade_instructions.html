Upgrade Instructions

Starting with v2.0.0 the iSphere project is open source. Unfortunately that required changing the plug-in IDs from de.taskforce to biz.isphere. Due to the new plug-in IDs you cannot upgrade to v2.0.0 as usually but need to uninstall your current iSphere plug-ins by hand.

Do not worry about your preferences. The preferences will automatically be moved to the new version.
