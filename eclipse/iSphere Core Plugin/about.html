The iSphere Project

The iSphere Open Source Project aims to deliver high quality extensions for WDSC, RDP and RDi to fill gaps and to ease a programmer's live. Although IBM's current Eclipse based Integrated Development Environment (IDE) is a great step forward compared to SEU, it still lacks a lot of features that are available on the green screen only. That is where the iSphere Project comes into play, trying to fill the gap.

Basically iSphere is driven from our ideas and inspirations but of course everybody is encouraged to contribute his ideas and manpower to improve the power of iSphere. We greatly appreciate any support regarding to development, translation, beta testing or documentation.

The iSphere project team.
